How to Give Using the QR Codes

Three steps, takes about a minute.

1

Scan the code

Open the QR code for the payment system you use. You can use your phone's camera or the app's built-in scanner.

2

Follow the prompts

Your phone will redirect you to the service. Enter the amount you want to give and confirm the transaction.

3

You're done

You'll receive an email confirmation of your donation for your personal records. Thank you for partnering with the ministry.

Give by Mail

Prefer to send a check?

If you'd rather give by mail, you can send a check to the church directly. Many of our long-time members give this way, and we welcome it.

Please make checks payable to Good News Missionary Baptist Church. Indicate "tithe," "offering," or a specific fund in the memo line if you'd like.
