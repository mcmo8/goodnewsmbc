What to Expect

Your first Sunday with us.

A few things to know before you walk through our doors at 77 West 1st Street.

Come as you are

You'll see plenty of Sunday best in our pews, and you'll see jeans and sneakers too. Wear what makes you comfortable. We're more interested in who's there than what's on.

Kids are welcome

Our Children and Youth Ministry has programming during Sunday School at 9:00 AM, and kids are welcome to stay with their family during Worship at 10:00 AM. Ushers can point you to where they need to be.

Parking and accessibility

Free parking is available in our lot off West 1st Street. Our sanctuary is ground-level with accessible seating. If you need assistance, our ushers are ready to help.
