Our Pastor

Pastor Tyrone L. Morrison II

"My calling is simple: love God, love His people, and lead them faithfully into the fullness of who Christ is."

Pastor Morrison was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, and accepted Christ at the age of eight. He united with Good News in 2011 and, on March 3, 2018, accepted the call to serve as Senior Pastor.

He shepherds Good News alongside his wife and First Lady, Doretha Morrison, with conviction, compassion, and a commitment to making the gospel plain.
